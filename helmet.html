Helmet and Visor

Helmets are made of clear, impact-resistant plastic, with interior padding for comfort.

Visors are covered with a thin layer of gold to help filter out harsh rays of sunlight.

The most important component is the velcro attached to the inside of the helmet, allowing astronauts to scratch their noses during long space walks.
